## What is it?
Apex Pathing is a passion project developed by multiple individuals from FIRST Tech Challenge teams around the world.
Our goal is to provide an advanced pathing experience for beginner programmers. We have support for many drivetrains 
such as mecanum, tank, kiwi, and swerve. All you need is a drivetrain, a localization solution, and a basic knowledge
of Java programming in FTC!


## What makes Apex Special?
Apex Pathing is versatile and highly compatible with other libraries. Our goal is to support a variety of path following
algorithms, allowing our users to mix and match different systems to perfect their autonomous routine. Some types of following systems
include but are not limited to: quintic splines, cubic splines, P2P pathing. We also aim to develop for wide range of drivetrains and localizers.

## What do you need?
* **Drivetrain:** Mecanum, swerve, tank, x-drive, or kiwi drive
* **Localization:** goBILDA Pinpoint, 2/3 dead wheels, or Sparkfun OTOS
* **Environment:** Android Studio or OnBotJava
