## What is it?
Apex Pathing is a passion project developed by multiple individuals from FIRST Tech Challenge teams around the world.
Our aim is to provide an advanced pathing experience for all FTC programmers, regardless of skill level. We have support
for multiple drivetrains such as mecanum, tank, kiwi, and swerve. All you need is a drivetrain, a localization solution, and
a basic knowledge of Java programming in FTC!

## When will it be released?
Apex Pathing is currently in the development stage and is not released to the public.
We're aiming to release our first version of Apex Pathing in the next few months, before the 2026-27 FTC season begins.
Join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/zQyJ5NCtD) to receive updates about Apex releases or to become a member of the dev team!

## What makes Apex special and different?
Apex Pathing is versatile and highly compatible with other libraries. Our goal is to support a variety of path following
algorithms, allowing our users to mix and match different systems to perfect their autonomous routine. Some types of following systems
include but are not limited to: quintic splines, cubic splines, P2P pathing. We are also compatible with a wide range of drivetrains and localizers.
We have other features still in development and some that are not ready to be announced yet, so stay tuned!

## Advantages of Apex Pathing
* **Extremely simple** and **easy to understand** docs
* **Simple and efficient** tuning process
* **Compatibility** with multiple localizers, drivetrains and On-Bot Java (soon)
* **Beginner friendly** approach to pathing
* **Multiple advanced** pathing algorithms to choose from
* **Accurate and precise** PDS controllers for optimal path following performance

## What do you need?
* **Drivetrain:** Mecanum Drive, Swerve Drive, Tank Drive, X-Drive or other
* **Localization:** goBILDA Pinpoint, 2 or 3 dead wheels, or Sparkfun OTOS
* **Environment:** Android Studio (On-Bot Java coming soon!)
